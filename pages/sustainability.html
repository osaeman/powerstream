Our Promise

We understand the impact of fuel consumption on both the environment and your bottom line.

A typical operation running on diesel can consume approximately 280,320 gallons annually. By switching to alternative fuels like Compressed Natural Gas (CNG), Natural Gas (NG), or Liquid Propane (LP), you can significantly reduce this consumption.

Switching to CNG or NG could conserve up to 243,528 gallons of fuel each year, while also offering substantial cost savings. LP offers similar benefits, conserving 280,320 gallons with significant operational savings.

Generator Rentals

Reduce fuel consumption and increase savings

When considering generator rentals, the type of fuel used can significantly impact your overall costs. The fuel consumption for a 500kW diesel generator is approximately 3 times higher than alternatives like CNG, NG, or LP. Choosing the right generator not only reduces your fuel consumption but also delivers significant savings on rental and operational expenses.
